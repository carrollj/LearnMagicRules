Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player cast a card from their graveyard for a specific cost and lets them tap a creature they control to reduce that cost. See rule [702.180](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-180), “Harmonize.”
