Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A zone in which not all players can be expected to see the cards’ faces. See rule [400.2](../../4-zones/400-general.md#rule-400-2). See also [Public Zone](../p/public-zone.md).
