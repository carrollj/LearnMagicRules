Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows a conspiracy card to be put into the command zone face down. See rule [702.106](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-106), “Hidden Agenda.”
