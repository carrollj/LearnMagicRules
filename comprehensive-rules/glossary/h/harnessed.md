## Definition

A designation permanents can have. Infinity permanents have their ∞ abilities as long as they are harnessed. See rule [701.64](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-64), “Harness,” and rule [702.186](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-186), “∞ (Infinity).”
