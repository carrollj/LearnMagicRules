## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a creature ignore the “summoning sickness” rule. See rule [702.10](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-10), “Haste,” and rule [302.6](../../3-card-types/302-creatures.md#rule-302-6).
