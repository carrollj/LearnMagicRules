## Definition

An ability found on the back faces of some double-faced cards. Cards with daybound and nightbound are face up when it’s day and face down when it’s night. See rule [702.145](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-145), “Daybound and Nightbound,” and rule [731](../../7-additional-rules/731-day-and-night.md#rule-731), “Day and Night.”
