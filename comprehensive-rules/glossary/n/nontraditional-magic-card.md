# Nontraditional Magic Card

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to N entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A card not included in players’ decks. It may be oversized or have a card back other than a “Deckmaster” back. See rule [108.2](../../1-game-concepts/108-cards.md#rule-108-2).
