Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

One kind of double-faced card. Nonmodal double-faced cards default to being cast with their front faces up but can transform to their back faces in some way. See rule [712](../../7-additional-rules/712-double-faced-cards.md#rule-712), “Double-Faced Cards.”
