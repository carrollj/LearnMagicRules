# Face a Villainous Choice

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that causes a player to choose one of two listed choices. See rule [701.55](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-55), “Face a Villainous Choice.”
