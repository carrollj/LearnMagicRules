Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a creature deal its combat damage before other creatures. See rule [702.7](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-7), “First Strike.”
