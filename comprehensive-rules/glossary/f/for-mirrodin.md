Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that creates a 2/2 red Rebel creature token and then attaches the Equipment with the ability to that token. See rule [702.163](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-163), “For Mirrodin!”
