Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To manipulate some of the cards on top of an opponent’s library. See rule [701.29](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-29), “Fateseal.”
