# Foretell

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player exile cards from their hand and cast them for an alternative cost on future turns. See rule [702.143](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-143), “Foretell.”
