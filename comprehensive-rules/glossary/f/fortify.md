## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player attach a Fortification to a land they control. See rule [301](../../3-card-types/301-artifacts.md#rule-301), “Artifacts,” and rule [702.67](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-67), “Fortify.”
