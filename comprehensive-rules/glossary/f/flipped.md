## Definition

A status a permanent may have. See rule [110.5](../../1-game-concepts/110-permanents.md#rule-110-5) and rule [710](../../7-additional-rules/710-flip-cards.md#rule-710), “Flip Cards.” See also [Unflipped](../u/unflipped.md).
