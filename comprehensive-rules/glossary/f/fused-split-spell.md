# Fused Split Spell

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to F entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A split card on the stack that has been cast using the fuse ability or a copy of such a card. See rule [702.102](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-102), “Fuse.”
