## Definition

1. A card is “face down” if it’s physically positioned so the card back is showing. Cards in some zones are normally kept face down. See section [4](../../4-zones/README.md), “Zones.”
2. A status a permanent may have. See rule [110.5](../../1-game-concepts/110-permanents.md#rule-110-5) and rule [702.37](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-37), “Morph.”
3. Face-down spells have additional rules. See rule [708](../../7-additional-rules/708-face-down-spells-and-permanents.md#rule-708), “Face-Down Spells and Permanents,” and rule [702.37](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-37), “Morph.”
