Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that reduces the cost of a spell based on the number of opponents you have. See rule [702.125](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-125), “Undaunted.”
