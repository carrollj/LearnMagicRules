Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An attacking creature once no creature has been declared as a blocker for it, unless an effect has caused it to become blocked. It remains an unblocked creature until it’s removed from combat or the combat phase ends, whichever comes first. See rule [509](../../5-turn-structure/509-declare-blockers-step.md#rule-509), “Declare Blockers Step.”
