Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player return a creature card from their graveyard to the battlefield. See rule [702.84](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-84), “Unearth.”
