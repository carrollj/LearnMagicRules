Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To rotate a permanent back to the upright position from a sideways position. See rule [701.26](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-26), “Tap and Untap.”
