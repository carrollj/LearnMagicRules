# Unattach

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to U entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To move an Equipment away from the creature it’s attached to so that the Equipment is on the battlefield but is not equipping anything. See rule [701.3d](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-3d).
