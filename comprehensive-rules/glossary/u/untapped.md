## Definition

A default status a permanent may have. See rule [110.5](../../1-game-concepts/110-permanents.md#rule-110-5) and rule [701.26](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-26), “Tap and Untap.” See also [Tapped](../t/tapped.md).
