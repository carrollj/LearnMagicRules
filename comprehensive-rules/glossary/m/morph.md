Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a card be cast face down as a 2/2 creature. See rule [702.37](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-37), “Morph,” and rule [708](../../7-additional-rules/708-face-down-spells-and-permanents.md#rule-708), “Face-Down Spells and Permanents.”
