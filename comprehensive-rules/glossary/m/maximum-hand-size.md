# Maximum Hand Size

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to M entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

The number of cards in hand a player must discard down to during their cleanup step. See rule [402.2](../../4-zones/402-hand.md#rule-402-2) and [514.1](../../5-turn-structure/514-cleanup-step.md#rule-514-1).
