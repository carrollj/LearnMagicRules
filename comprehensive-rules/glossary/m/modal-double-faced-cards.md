Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

One kind of double-faced card. Modal double-faced cards can be played with either of their two faces up. See rule [712](../../7-additional-rules/712-double-faced-cards.md#rule-712), “Double-Faced Cards.”
