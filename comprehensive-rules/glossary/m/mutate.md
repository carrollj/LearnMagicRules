Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword that lets a creature card be cast as a mutating creature spell. See rule [702.140](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-140), “Mutate.”
