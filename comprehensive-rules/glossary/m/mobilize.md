# Mobilize

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that creates a number of tapped and attacking red Warrior creature tokens that are sacrificed at the beginning of the next end step. See rule [702.181](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-181), “Mobilize.”
