# Manifest Dread

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to M entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that puts one of the top two cards of your library onto the battlefield face down as a 2/2 creature. See rule [701.62](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-62), “Manifest Dread,” rule [701.40](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-40), “Manifest,” and rule [708](../../7-additional-rules/708-face-down-spells-and-permanents.md#rule-708), “Face-Down Spells and Permanents.”
