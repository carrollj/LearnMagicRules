Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that puts +1/+1 counters on a creature and makes it become monstrous. See rule [701.37](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-37), “Monstrosity.”
