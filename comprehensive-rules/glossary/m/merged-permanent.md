Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A card or token may merge with a permanent to form a merged permanent. This merged permanent is represented by more than one card and/or token. See rule [730](../../7-additional-rules/730-merging-with-permanents.md#rule-730), “Merging with Permanents.”
