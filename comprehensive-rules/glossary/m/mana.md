## Definition

The primary resource in the game. It is spent to pay costs, usually when casting spells and activating abilities. See rule [106](../../1-game-concepts/106-mana.md#rule-106), “Mana,” rule [107.4](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-4), and rule [202](../../2-parts-of-a-card/202-mana-cost-and-color.md#rule-202), “Mana Cost and Color.”
