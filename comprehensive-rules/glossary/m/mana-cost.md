## Definition

A characteristic, and part of a card. A card’s mana cost is indicated by the mana symbols printed in its upper right corner. See rule [107.4](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-4) and rule [202](../../2-parts-of-a-card/202-mana-cost-and-color.md#rule-202), “Mana Cost and Color.”
