Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Part of the turn. The first, or precombat, main phase is the second phase of the turn. The second, or postcombat, main phase is the fourth phase of the turn. See rule [505](../../5-turn-structure/505-main-phase.md#rule-505), “Main Phase.”
