# More Than Meets the Eye

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to M entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows some cards to be cast converted. See rule [702.162](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-162), “More Than Meets the Eye,” and rule [701.28](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-28), “Convert.”
