# Mayhem

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows you to play cards you discarded this turn from your graveyard for an alternative cost. See rule [702.187](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-187), “Mayhem.”
