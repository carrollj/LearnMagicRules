Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. A zone. A player’s graveyard is their discard pile.
2. All the cards in a player’s graveyard.
See rule [404](../../4-zones/404-graveyard.md#rule-404), “Graveyard.”
