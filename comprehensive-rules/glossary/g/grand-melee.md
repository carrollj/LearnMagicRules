Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A multiplayer variant in which a large group of players (usually ten or more) compete as individuals against each other. See rule [807](../../8-multiplayer-rules/807-grand-melee-variant.md#rule-807), “Grand Melee Variant.”
