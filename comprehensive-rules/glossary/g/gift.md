Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows a spell’s caster to choose an opponent as to receive a benefit as the spell resolves or when the permanent with the ability enters. See rule [702.174](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-174), “Gift.”
