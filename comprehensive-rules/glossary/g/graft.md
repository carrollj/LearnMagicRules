# Graft

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that has a permanent enter the battlefield with +1/+1 counters on it and can move those counters to other creatures. See rule [702.58](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-58), “Graft.”
