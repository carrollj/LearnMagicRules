# Goad

[Back to G entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that causes a creature to be goaded until a player’s next turn. See rule [701.15](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-15), “Goad.”
