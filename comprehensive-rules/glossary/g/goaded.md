Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A designation a permanent can have. Goaded creatures are forced to attack and to attack a player other than the player that caused it to be goaded. See rule [701.15](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-15), “Goad.”
