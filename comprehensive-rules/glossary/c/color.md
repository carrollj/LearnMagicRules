Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. A characteristic of an object. See rule [105](../../1-game-concepts/105-colors.md#rule-105), “Colors,” and rule [202](../../2-parts-of-a-card/202-mana-cost-and-color.md#rule-202), “Mana Cost and Color.”
2. An attribute mana may have. See rule [106](../../1-game-concepts/106-mana.md#rule-106), “Mana.”
