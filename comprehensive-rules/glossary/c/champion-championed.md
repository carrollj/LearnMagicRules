## Definition

“Champion” is a keyword ability that lets one creature temporarily replace another. A permanent is “championed” by another permanent if the latter exiles the former as the direct result of a champion ability. See rule [702.72](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-72), “Champion.”
