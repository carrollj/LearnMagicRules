# Compleated

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes a planeswalker to enter the battlefield with fewer loyalty counters if a player chose to pay life for Phyrexian mana symbols in its cost. See rule [702.150](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-150), “Compleated.”
