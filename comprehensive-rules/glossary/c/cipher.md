Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows you to encode a card on a creature and cast that card whenever that creature deals combat damage to a player. See rule [702.99](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-99), “Cipher.”
