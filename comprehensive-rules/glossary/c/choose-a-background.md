Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A variant of the partner ability that lets two legendary permanent cards be your commander in the Commander variant rather than one if one of them has the “choose a Background” ability and the other is a Background enchantment card. See rule [702.124](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-124), “Partner,” and rule [903](../../9-casual-variants/903-commander.md#rule-903), “Commander.”
