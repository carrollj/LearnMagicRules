Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To quit the game. Conceding a game immediately causes that player to leave that game and lose that game. See rule [104](../../1-game-concepts/104-ending-the-game.md#rule-104), “Ending the Game.”
