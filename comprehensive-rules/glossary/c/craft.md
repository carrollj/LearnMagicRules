## Definition

Craft is an activated ability that allows a player to exile cards from their graveyard and/or permanents they control to exile the permanent with the craft ability and return it onto the battlefield transformed. See rule [702.167](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-167), “Craft.”
