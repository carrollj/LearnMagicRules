Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you tap creatures rather than pay mana to cast a spell. See rule [702.51](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-51), “Convoke.”
