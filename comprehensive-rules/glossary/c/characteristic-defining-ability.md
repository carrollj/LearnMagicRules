Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A kind of static ability that conveys information about an object’s characteristics that would normally be found elsewhere on that object (such as in its mana cost, type line, or power/toughness box). See rule [604.3](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/604-handling-static-abilities.md#rule-604-3).
