## Definition

A keyword action that puts a card onto the battlefield face down as a 2/2 creature with ward {2}. See rule [701.58](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-58), “Cloak,” and rule [708](../../7-additional-rules/708-face-down-spells-and-permanents.md#rule-708), “Face-Down Spells and Permanents.”
