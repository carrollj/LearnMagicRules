Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A designation a player can have. The ascend keyword causes a player to get this designation once they control ten permanents. See rule [702.131](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-131), “Ascend.”
