# Control Another Player

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To make all choices and decisions that player is allowed to make, or is told to make, by rules or by any objects. See rule [723](../../7-additional-rules/723-controlling-another-player.md#rule-723), “Controlling Another Player.”
