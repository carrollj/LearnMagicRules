Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows you to pay an alternative cost to remove some of a spell’s text. See rule [702.148](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-148), “Cleave.”
