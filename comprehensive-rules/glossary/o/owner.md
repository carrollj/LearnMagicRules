## Definition

The player who (for purposes of the game) a card, permanent, token, or spell belongs to. See rules [108.3](../../1-game-concepts/108-cards.md#rule-108-3), [110.2](../../1-game-concepts/110-permanents.md#rule-110-2), [111.2](../../1-game-concepts/111-tokens.md#rule-111-2), and [112.2](../../1-game-concepts/112-spells.md#rule-112-2).
