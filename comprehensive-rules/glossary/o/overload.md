Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows a spell to affect either a single target or many objects. See rule [702.96](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-96), “Overload.”
