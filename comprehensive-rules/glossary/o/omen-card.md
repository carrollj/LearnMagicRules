Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Cards with a two-part card frame (one part of which is inset on the left) on a single card where the alternative characteristics include the Omen spell type. See rule [720](../../7-additional-rules/720-omen-cards.md#rule-720), “Omen Cards.”
