Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An effect that does something just once and doesn’t have a duration. See rule [610](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/610-one-shot-effects.md#rule-610), “One-Shot Effects.” See also Continuous Effects.
