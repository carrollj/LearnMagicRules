## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you put a +1/+1 counter on a creature whenever you cast a spell if the amount of mana you spent on it is greater than that creature’s power or toughness. See rule [702.191](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-191), “Increment.”
