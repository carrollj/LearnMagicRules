Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. An artifact subtype.
2. ∞ is a keyword found on Infinity cards that grants an ability as long as that permanent is harnessed. See rule [702.186](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-186), “∞ (Infinity),” and rule [701.64](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-64), “Harness.”
