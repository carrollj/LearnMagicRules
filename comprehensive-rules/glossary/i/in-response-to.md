## Definition

An instant spell that’s been cast, or an activated ability that’s been activated, while another spell or ability is on the stack has been cast or activated “in response to” the earlier spell or ability. See rule [117.7](../../1-game-concepts/117-timing-and-priority.md#rule-117-7).
