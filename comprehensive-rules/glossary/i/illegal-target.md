# Illegal Target

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to I entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A target that no longer exists or no longer meets the specifications stated by the spell or ability that’s targeting it. See rule [608.2b](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/608-resolving-spells-and-abilities.md#rule-608-2b).
