Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that creates a 1/1 colorless Hero creature token and then attaches the Equipment with the ability to that token. See rule [702.182](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-182), “Job Select.”
