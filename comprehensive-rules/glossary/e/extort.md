Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you gain life and have opponents lose life whenever you cast a spell. See rule [702.101](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-101), “Extort.”
