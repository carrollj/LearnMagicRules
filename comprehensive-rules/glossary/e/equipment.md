## Definition

An artifact subtype. Equipment can be attached to creatures. See rule [301](../../3-card-types/301-artifacts.md#rule-301), “Artifacts,” and rule [702.6](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-6), “Equip.”
