Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player choose all modes for a spell rather than just the number specified. See rule [702.42](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-42), “Entwine.”
