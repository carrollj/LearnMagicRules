Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability on some modal spells that adds a cost for choosing additional modes. See rule [702.120](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-120), “Escalate.”
