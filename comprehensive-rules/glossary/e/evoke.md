Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes a permanent to be sacrificed when it enters the battlefield. See rule [702.74](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-74), “Evoke.”
