## Definition

A keyword ability that defines what an Aura spell can target and what an Aura permanent can be attached to. See rule [303](../../3-card-types/303-enchantments.md#rule-303), “Enchantments,” and rule [702.5](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-5), “Enchant.”
