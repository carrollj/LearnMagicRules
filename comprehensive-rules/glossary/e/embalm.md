Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player exile a creature card from their graveyard to create a mummified token version of that card. See rule [702.128](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-128), “Embalm.”
