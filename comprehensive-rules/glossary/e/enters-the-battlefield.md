Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A nontoken permanent enters the battlefield when it’s moved onto the battlefield from another zone. A token enters the battlefield as it’s created. See rules [403.3](../../4-zones/403-battlefield.md#rule-403-3), [603.6a](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-6a), [603.6d](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-6d), and [614.12](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/614-replacement-effects.md#rule-614-12). This phrase has been shortened to simply “enters” in rules text on cards in most contexts.
