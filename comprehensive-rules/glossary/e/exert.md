Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that stops a permanent from untapping during the next untap step of the player who exerted it. See rule [701.43](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-43), “Exert.”
