Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you put a +1/+1 counter on a creature when a larger creature enters the battlefield under your control. See rule [702.100](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-100), “Evolve.”
