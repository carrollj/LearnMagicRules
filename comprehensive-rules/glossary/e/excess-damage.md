# Excess Damage

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to E entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Damage dealt to a creature greater than what would be lethal damage or damage dealt to a planeswalker greater than its loyalty. See rule [120.4a](../../1-game-concepts/120-damage.md#rule-120-4a).
