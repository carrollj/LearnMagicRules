# Summon (Obsolete)

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Older creature cards were printed with “Summon [creature type]” on their type lines. All such cards have received errata in the Oracle card reference to say “Creature — [creature type].” (Many of these cards’ creature types have also been updated.) See [Creature](../c/creature.md).
