# Start Your Engines!

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that gives a player a speed of 1 if they have no speed. Once a player has speed, their speed increases once on each of their turns when an opponent loses life. See rule [702.179](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-179), “Start Your Engines!”
