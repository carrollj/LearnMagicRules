## Definition

A kind of ability. Static abilities do something all the time rather than being activated or triggered. See rule [113](../../1-game-concepts/113-abilities.md#rule-113), “Abilities,” and rule [604](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/604-handling-static-abilities.md#rule-604), “Handling Static Abilities.”
