# Summoning Sickness Rule

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Informal term for a player’s inability to attack with a creature or to activate its abilities that include the tap symbol or the untap symbol unless the creature has been under that player’s control since the beginning of that player’s most recent turn. See rule [302.6](../../3-card-types/302-creatures.md#rule-302-6). See also [Haste](../h/haste.md).
