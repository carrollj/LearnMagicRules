Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A game supplement with a Magic card back that can be used to represent a double-faced card or meld card. See rule [713](../../7-additional-rules/713-substitute-cards.md#rule-713), “Substitute Cards.”
