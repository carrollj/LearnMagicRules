Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that can have a permanent enter the battlefield with +1/+1 counters or charge counters on it. See rule [702.44](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-44), “Sunburst.”
