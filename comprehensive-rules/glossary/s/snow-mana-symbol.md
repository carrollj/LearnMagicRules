## Definition

The snow mana symbol {S} represents a cost that can be paid with one mana produced by a snow source. It can also represent mana from a snow source that was spent to pay a cost. See rule [107.4h](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-4h).
