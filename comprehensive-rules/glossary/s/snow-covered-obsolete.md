Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Some older cards were printed with the term “snow-covered” in their rules text. Except when referencing card names, such cards have received errata in the Oracle card reference to reference the supertype “snow” instead. See [Snow](snow.md).
