Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An option that may be used in certain multiplayer variants, such as Two-Headed Giant and Archenemy. See rule [805](../../8-multiplayer-rules/805-shared-team-turns-option.md#rule-805), “Shared Team Turns Option.”
