# Saddle

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you tap creatures to make another creature “saddled” until end of turn. See rule [702.171](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-171), “Saddle.”
