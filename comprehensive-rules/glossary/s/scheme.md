Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A card type seen only on nontraditional Magic cards in the Archenemy casual variant. A scheme card is not a permanent. See rule [314](../../3-card-types/314-schemes.md#rule-314), “Schemes.”
