# Surge

[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that provides an alternative cost to cast a card if you or one of your teammates has cast another spell in the same turn. See rule [702.117](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-117), “Surge.”
