Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A characteristic that appears before the card type on a card’s type line. Most cards don’t have a supertype. See rule [205.4](../../2-parts-of-a-card/205-type-line.md#rule-205-4), “Supertypes.”
