# Siege

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A battle subtype. Only an opponent of a Siege’s controller can be its protector. When the last defense counter is removed, its controller exiles the Siege, then they may cast it transformed without paying its mana cost. See rule [310.11](../../3-card-types/310-battles.md#rule-310-11).
