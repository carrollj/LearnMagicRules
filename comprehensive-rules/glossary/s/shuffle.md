Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To randomize the cards in a deck (before a game) or library (during a game). See rule [103.3](../../1-game-concepts/103-starting-the-game.md#rule-103-3) and rule [701.24](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-24).
