Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

“Set [something] aside” is an obsolete term for “exile [something].” Cards with that text have received errata in the Oracle card reference. See [Exile](../e/exile.md).
