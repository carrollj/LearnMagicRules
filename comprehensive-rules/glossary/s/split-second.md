# Split Second

[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that makes it nearly impossible for a player to respond to a spell. See rule [702.61](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-61), “Split Second.”
