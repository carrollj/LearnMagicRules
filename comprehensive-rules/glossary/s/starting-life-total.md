Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

The amount of life a player has as a game begins. In most games, each player’s starting life total is 20. See rule [103.4](../../1-game-concepts/103-starting-the-game.md#rule-103-4).
