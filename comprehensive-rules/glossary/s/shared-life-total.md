## Definition

In the Two-Headed Giant multiplayer variant, each team has a “shared life total” rather than each player having an individual life total. See rule [810](../../8-multiplayer-rules/810-two-headed-giant-variant.md#rule-810), “Two-Headed Giant Variant.”
