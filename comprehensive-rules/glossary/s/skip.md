## Definition

Effects that use the word “skip” are replacement effects. The word “skip” indicates what events, steps, phases, or turns will be replaced with nothing. See rule [614](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/614-replacement-effects.md#rule-614), “Replacement Effects.”
