# Scavenge

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows you to exile a creature card from your graveyard to put +1/+1 counters on a creature. See rule [702.97](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-97), “Scavenge.”
