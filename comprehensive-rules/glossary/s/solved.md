# Solved

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to S entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. A designation a Case may have, allowing its last ability to affect the game. See rule [719](../../7-additional-rules/719-case-cards.md#rule-719), “Case Cards.”
2. A keyword ability of Case cards that affects the game only if the Case has the solved designation. See rule [702.169](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-169), “Solved.”
