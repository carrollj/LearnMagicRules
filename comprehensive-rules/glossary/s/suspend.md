## Definition

A keyword ability that provides an alternative way to play a card. See rule [702.62](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-62), “Suspend.” A card is “suspended” if it’s in the exile zone, has suspend, and has a time counter on it.
