## Definition

A creature “blocks alone” if it’s the only creature declared as a blocker during the declare blockers step. A creature “is blocking alone” if it’s blocking but no other creatures are. See rule [506.5](../../5-turn-structure/506-combat-phase.md#rule-506-5).
