## Definition

An attacking creature that another creature blocks or that an effect causes to become blocked. It remains a blocked creature until it’s removed from combat, an effect says that it becomes unblocked, or the combat phase ends, whichever comes first. See rule [509](../../5-turn-structure/509-declare-blockers-step.md#rule-509), “Declare Blockers Step.”
