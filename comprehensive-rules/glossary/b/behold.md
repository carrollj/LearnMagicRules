## Definition

A keyword action that allows a player to choose a permanent they control of a particular quality or reveal a card of that quality from their hand, usually to pay a cost or get an additional effect. See rule [701.4](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-4), “Behold.”
