## Definition

An enchantment type that, in combination with the “choose a Background” ability, may allow a legendary enchantment card to be your commander. See rule [702.124](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-124), “Partner,” and rule [903](../../9-casual-variants/903-commander.md#rule-903), “Commander.”
