Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Banding is a keyword ability that modifies the rules for declaring attackers and assigning combat damage. “Bands with other” is a specialized version of the ability. See rule [702.22](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-22), “Banding.”
