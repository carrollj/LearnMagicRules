Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To choose a set of creatures that will attack, declare whether each creature is attacking the defending player or a planeswalker that player controls, and pay any costs required to allow those creatures to attack. See rule [508.1](../../5-turn-structure/508-declare-attackers-step.md#rule-508-1).
