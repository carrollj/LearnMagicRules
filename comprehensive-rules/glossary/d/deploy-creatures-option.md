Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An option that may be used in certain multiplayer variants to pass control of creatures between teammates. See rule [804](../../8-multiplayer-rules/804-deploy-creatures-option.md#rule-804), “Deploy Creatures Option.”
