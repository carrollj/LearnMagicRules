# Defending Player

[Back to D entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

The player who can be attacked, and whose planeswalkers can be attacked, during the combat phase. See rule [506.2](../../5-turn-structure/506-combat-phase.md#rule-506-2). In certain multiplayer games, there may be more than one defending player; see rule [802](../../8-multiplayer-rules/802-attack-multiple-players-option.md#rule-802), “Attack Multiple Players Option,” and rule [805.10](../../8-multiplayer-rules/805-shared-team-turns-option.md#rule-805-10).
