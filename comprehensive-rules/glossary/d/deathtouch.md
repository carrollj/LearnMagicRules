Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes damage dealt by an object to be especially effective. See rule [702.2](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-2), “Deathtouch.”
