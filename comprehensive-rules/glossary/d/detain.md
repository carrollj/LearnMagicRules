Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that temporarily stops a permanent from attacking, blocking, or having its activated abilities activated. See rule [701.35](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-35), “Detain.”
