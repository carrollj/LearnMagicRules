# Delayed Triggered Ability

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to D entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An ability created by effects generated when some spells or abilities resolve, or when some replacement effects are applied, that does something later on rather than at that time. See rule [603.7](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-7).
