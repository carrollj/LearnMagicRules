Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A triggered ability found on some spells that let its controller copy it and choose an opponent to also copy it. See rule [702.144](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-144), “Demonstrate.”
