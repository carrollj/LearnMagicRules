Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that puts a +1/+1 counter on a creature when it attacks the player with the most life. See rule [702.105](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-105), “Dethrone.”
