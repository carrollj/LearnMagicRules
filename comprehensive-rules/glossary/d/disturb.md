Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows a player to cast a double-faced card transformed from the graveyard. See rule [702.146](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-146), “Disturb.”
