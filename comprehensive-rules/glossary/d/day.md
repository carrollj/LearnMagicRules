## Definition

Along with night, a designation the game can have. See rule [731](../../7-additional-rules/731-day-and-night.md#rule-731), “Day and Night,” and rule [702.145](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-145), “Daybound and Nightbound.”
