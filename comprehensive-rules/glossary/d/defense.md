Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. Part of a card that only battle cards have. A battle card’s defense is printed in its lower right corner. See rule [210](../../2-parts-of-a-card/210-defense.md#rule-210), “Defense.”
2. A characteristic that only battles can have. See rule [310](../../3-card-types/310-battles.md#rule-310), “Battles.”
