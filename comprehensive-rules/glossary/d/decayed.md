# Decayed

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that means “This creature can’t block” and “When this creature attacks, sacrifice it at end of combat. See rule [702.147](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-147), “Decayed.”
