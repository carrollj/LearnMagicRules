## Definition

The collection of cards a player starts the game with; it becomes that player’s library. See rule [100](../../1-game-concepts/100-general.md#rule-100), “General,” and rule [103](../../1-game-concepts/103-starting-the-game.md#rule-103), “Starting the Game.”
