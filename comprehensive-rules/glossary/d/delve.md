Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you exile cards from your graveyard rather than pay generic mana to cast a spell. See rule [702.66](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-66), “Delve.”
