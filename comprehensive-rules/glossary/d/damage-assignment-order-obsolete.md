# Damage Assignment Order (Obsolete)

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to D entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Previously, if a creature blocks or becomes blocked by multiple creatures, the creature’s controller would be required to choose an order in which it would assign combat damage to the creatures blocking or blocked by it. Now, its controller no longer needs to assign an order, and simply divides its combat damage as they choose among all creatures it’s blocking or blocked by. See rules [510.1c](../../5-turn-structure/510-combat-damage-step.md#rule-510-1c)-d.
