## Definition

A system that may be used to determine in which order continuous effects in the same layer or sublayer are applied. See rule [613.8](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/613-interaction-of-continuous-effects.md#rule-613-8). See also [Timestamp Order](../t/timestamp-order.md).
