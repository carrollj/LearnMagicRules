## Definition

Part of a draft in which each player opens an unopened booster pack and the cards in those booster packs are drafted. See rules [905.1a](../../9-casual-variants/905-conspiracy-draft.md#rule-905-1a) and [905.1b](../../9-casual-variants/905-conspiracy-draft.md#rule-905-1b).
