## Definition

An artifact subtype. Vehicles can become artifact creatures. See rule [301](../../3-card-types/301-artifacts.md#rule-301), “Artifacts,” and rule [702.122](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-122), “Crew.”
