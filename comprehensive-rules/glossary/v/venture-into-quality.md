# Venture into [Quality]

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to V entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A variant of the venture into the dungeon ability that allows a player to bring a dungeon card with [quality] into the game or move a player’s venture marker. See rule [701.49](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-49), “Venture into the Dungeon.”
