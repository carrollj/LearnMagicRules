Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An obsolete creature type. Cards printed with this subtype have been given errata in the Oracle card reference so they have the legendary supertype instead. See [Legendary](legendary.md).
