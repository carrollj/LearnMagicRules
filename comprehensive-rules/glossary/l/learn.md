Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that lets a player add a Lesson card to their hand from outside the game or discard a card to draw a card. See rule [701.48](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-48), “Learn.”
