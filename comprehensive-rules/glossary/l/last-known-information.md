# Last Known Information

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to L entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Information about an object that’s no longer in the zone it’s expected to be in, or information about a player who’s no longer in the game. This information captures that object’s last existence in that zone or that player’s last existence in the game. See rules [113.7a](../../1-game-concepts/113-abilities.md#rule-113-7a), [608.2b](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/608-resolving-spells-and-abilities.md#rule-608-2b), [608.2h](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/608-resolving-spells-and-abilities.md#rule-608-2h), and [800.4h](../../8-multiplayer-rules/800-general.md#rule-800-4h).
