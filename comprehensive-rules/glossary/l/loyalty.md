# Loyalty

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to L entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. Part of a card that only planeswalker cards have. A planeswalker card’s loyalty is printed in its lower right corner. See rule [209](../../2-parts-of-a-card/209-loyalty.md#rule-209), “Loyalty.”
2. A characteristic that only planeswalkers have. See rule [306.5](../../3-card-types/306-planeswalkers.md#rule-306-5).
