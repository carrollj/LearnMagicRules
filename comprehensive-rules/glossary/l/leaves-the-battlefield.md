# Leaves the Battlefield

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to L entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A permanent “leaves the battlefield” when it’s moved from the battlefield to another zone, or (if it’s phased in) when it leaves the game because its owner leaves the game. See rules [603.6c](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-6c) and [603.10](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-10).
