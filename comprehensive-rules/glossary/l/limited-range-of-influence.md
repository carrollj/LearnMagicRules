Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An optional rule used in some multiplayer games that limits what a player can affect. See rule [801](../../8-multiplayer-rules/801-limited-range-of-influence-option.md#rule-801), “Limited Range of Influence Option.”
