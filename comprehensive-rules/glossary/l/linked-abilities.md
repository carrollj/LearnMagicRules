[Back to L entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Two abilities printed on the same object such that one of them causes actions to be taken or objects to be affected and the other one directly refers to those actions or objects. See rule [607](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/607-linked-abilities.md#rule-607), “Linked Abilities.”
