Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A symbol that represents a keyword ability indicating abilities, power, and toughness a leveler card may have. See rule [107.8](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-8) and rule [711](../../7-additional-rules/711-leveler-cards.md#rule-711), “Leveler Cards.”
