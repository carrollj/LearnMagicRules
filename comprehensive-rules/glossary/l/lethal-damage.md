# Lethal Damage

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to L entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An amount of damage greater than or equal to a creature’s toughness. See rules [120.4a](../../1-game-concepts/120-damage.md#rule-120-4a), [120.6](../../1-game-concepts/120-damage.md#rule-120-6), [510.1](../../5-turn-structure/510-combat-damage-step.md#rule-510-1), and [704.5g](../../7-additional-rules/704-state-based-actions.md#rule-704-5g).
