Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A generic term for a group of keyword abilities that restrict whether a creature may be blocked. See rule [702.14](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-14), “Landwalk.”
