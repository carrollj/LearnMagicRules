## Definition

The first part of a triggered ability, consisting of “when,” “whenever,” or “at” followed by a trigger event. See rule [603](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603), “Handling Triggered Abilities.”
