# Time Travel

[Back to T entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To add a time counter to or remove a time counter from any number of permanents you control with a time counter on them or suspended cards you own in exile with time counters on them. See rule [701.56](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-56), “Time Travel.”
