# The Ring Tempts You

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to T entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that causes a player to choose a creature they control to be their legendary Ring-bearer and may cause them to create an emblem called The Ring. See rule [701.54](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-54), “The Ring Tempts You.”
