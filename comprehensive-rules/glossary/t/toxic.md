# Toxic

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes a player to get poison counters due to combat damage from creatures with the ability. See rule [702.164](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-164), “Toxic.”
