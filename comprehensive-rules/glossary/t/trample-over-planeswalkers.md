# Trample Over Planeswalkers

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to T entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A variant of trample that modifies how a creature assigns combat damage if it’s attacking a planeswalker. See rule [702.19](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-19), “Trample”
