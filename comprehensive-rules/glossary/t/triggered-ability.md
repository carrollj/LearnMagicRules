## Definition

A kind of ability. Triggered abilities begin with the word “when,” “whenever,” or “at.” They’re written as “[Trigger condition], [effect].” See rule [113](../../1-game-concepts/113-abilities.md#rule-113), “Abilities,” and rule [603](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603), “Handling Triggered Abilities.”
