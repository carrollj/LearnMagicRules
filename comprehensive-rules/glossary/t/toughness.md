# Toughness

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to T entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. Part of a card that only creature cards have. A creature card’s toughness is printed after the slash in its lower right corner. See rule [208](../../2-parts-of-a-card/208-power-toughness.md#rule-208), “Power/Toughness.”
2. A characteristic that only creatures have. See rule [302.4](../../3-card-types/302-creatures.md#rule-302-4).
