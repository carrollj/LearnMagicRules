## Definition

A group of players who share a common victory condition in a multiplayer game. See rule [808](../../8-multiplayer-rules/808-team-vs-team-variant.md#rule-808), “Team vs. Team Variant,” rule [809](../../8-multiplayer-rules/809-emperor-variant.md#rule-809), “Emperor Variant,” rule [810](../../8-multiplayer-rules/810-two-headed-giant-variant.md#rule-810), “Two-Headed Giant Variant,” and rule [811](../../8-multiplayer-rules/811-alternating-teams-variant.md#rule-811), “Alternating Teams Variant.”
