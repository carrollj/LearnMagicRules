Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A multiplayer variant played among two-player teams that each have a shared life total and take a simultaneous turn. See rule [810](../../8-multiplayer-rules/810-two-headed-giant-variant.md#rule-810), “Two-Headed Giant Variant.”
