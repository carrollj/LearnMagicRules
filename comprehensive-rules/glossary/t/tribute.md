Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows an opponent to choose between a creature entering the battlefield with +1/+1 counters or an additional ability. See rule [702.104](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-104), “Tribute.”
