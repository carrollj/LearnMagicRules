Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

Game actions that happen automatically when certain steps or phases begin, or when each step or phase ends. See rule [703](../../7-additional-rules/703-turn-based-actions.md#rule-703), “Turn-Based Actions.”
