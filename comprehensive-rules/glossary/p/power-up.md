Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A one-time activated ability found on permanents that costs less during the turn the permanent with the ability entered the battlefield. See rule [702.193](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-193), “Power-up.”
