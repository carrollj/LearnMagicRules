# Phased In, Phased Out

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to P entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A status a permanent may have. Phased-in is the default status. Phased-out permanents are treated as though they do not exist. See rule [110.5](../../1-game-concepts/110-permanents.md#rule-110-5) and rule [702.26](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-26), “Phasing.” (“Phased-out” was a zone in older versions of the rules.)
