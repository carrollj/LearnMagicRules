Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A card type seen only on nontraditional Magic cards in the Planechase casual variant. A phenomenon card is not a permanent. See rule [312](../../3-card-types/312-phenomena.md#rule-312), “Phenomena.”
