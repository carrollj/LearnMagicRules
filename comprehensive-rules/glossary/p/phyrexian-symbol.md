Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A symbol used in rules text to represent any of the five Phyrexian mana symbols. See rule [107.4g](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-4g).
