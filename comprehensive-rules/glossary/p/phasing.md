Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes a permanent to sometimes be treated as though it does not exist. See rule [702.26](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-26), “Phasing.”
