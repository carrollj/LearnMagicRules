# Planeswalk

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to P entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To put each face-up plane card or phenomenon card on the bottom of its owner’s planar deck face down, then move the top card of your planar deck off that planar deck and turn it face up in a Planechase game. See rule [701.31](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-31), “Planeswalk.”
