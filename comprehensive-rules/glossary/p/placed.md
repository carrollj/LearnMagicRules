## Definition

(Obsolete) Some spells and abilities previously referred to a counter being “placed” on a permanent. These cards have received errata in the Oracle card reference to use the term “put” instead. Due to a rules change, these cards continue to function as they did before. See rule [122](../../1-game-concepts/122-counters.md#rule-122), “Counters.”
