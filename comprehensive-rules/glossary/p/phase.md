Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. A subsection of a turn. See section [5](../../5-turn-structure/README.md), “Turn Structure.”
2. A permanent “phases in” when its status changes from phased out to phased in. A permanent “phases out” when its status changes from phased in to phased out. See rule [702.26](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-26), “Phasing.”
