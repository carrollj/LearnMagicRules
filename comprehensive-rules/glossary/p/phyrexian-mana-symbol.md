Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A mana symbol that represents a cost that can be paid either by spending colored mana or by paying life. See rule [107.4](../../1-game-concepts/107-numbers-and-symbols.md#rule-107-4).
