Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that causes a creature to get +1/+1 whenever its controller casts a noncreature spell. See rule [702.108](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-108), “Prowess.”
