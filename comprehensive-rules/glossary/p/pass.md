Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To decline to take any action (such as casting a spell or activating an ability) when you have priority. See rule [117](../../1-game-concepts/117-timing-and-priority.md#rule-117), “Timing and Priority.”
