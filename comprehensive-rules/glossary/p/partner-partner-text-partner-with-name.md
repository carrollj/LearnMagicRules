# Partner, “Partner—[text],” “Partner with [name]”

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets two legendary cards be your commander in the Commander variant rather than one. “Partner with [name]” is a specialized version of the ability that works even outside of the Commander variant to help two cards reach the battlefield together. See rule [702.124](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-124), “Partner,” and rule [903](../../9-casual-variants/903-commander.md#rule-903), “Commander.”
