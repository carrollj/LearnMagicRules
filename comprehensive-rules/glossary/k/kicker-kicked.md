## Definition

Kicker is a keyword ability that represents an optional additional cost. A spell has been kicked if its controller declared the intention to pay any or all of its kicker costs. See rule [702.33](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-33), “Kicker.”
