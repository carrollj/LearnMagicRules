Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A rules term used to describe a creature that’s legendary, not a Villain, and red and/or white. See rule [700.16](../../7-additional-rules/700-general.md#rule-700-16).
