## Definition

A keyword action. “Waterbend [cost]” means to pay that cost and, for each {1} in that cost, you may tap an untapped artifact or creature you control rather than pay mana. See rule [701.67](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-67), “Waterbend.”
