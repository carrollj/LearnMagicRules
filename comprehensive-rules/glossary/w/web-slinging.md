Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows spells to be cast for an alternative cost by returning a tapped creature you control to its owner’s hand. See rule [702.188](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-188), “Web-slinging.”
