Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A state-based action that causes all permanents with the world supertype except the one that has had the world supertype for the shortest amount of time are put into their owners’ graveyards. See rule [704.5k](../../7-additional-rules/704-state-based-actions.md#rule-704-5k).
