## Definition

An effect that precludes one or more creatures from attacking or blocking. See rules [508.1c](../../5-turn-structure/508-declare-attackers-step.md#rule-508-1c) and [509.1b](../../5-turn-structure/509-declare-blockers-step.md#rule-509-1b).
