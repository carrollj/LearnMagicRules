# Redirection Effect

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to R entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A kind of replacement effect that causes damage that would be dealt to one creature, planeswalker, or player to be dealt instead to another creature, planeswalker, or player. See rule [614.9](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/614-replacement-effects.md#rule-614-9).
