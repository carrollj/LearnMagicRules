# Rad Counter

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to R entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A type of counter a player can have that causes that player to mill cards at the beginning of their precombat main phase, then lose 1 life and remove one rad counter for each nonland card milled this way. See rule [122](../../1-game-concepts/122-counters.md#rule-122), “Counters,” and rule [728](../../7-additional-rules/728-rad-counters.md#rule-728), “Rad Counters.”
