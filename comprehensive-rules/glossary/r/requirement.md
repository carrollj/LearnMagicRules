## Definition

An effect that forces one or more creatures to attack or block. See rules [508.1d](../../5-turn-structure/508-declare-attackers-step.md#rule-508-1d) and [509.1c](../../5-turn-structure/509-declare-blockers-step.md#rule-509-1c).
