Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows an instant or sorcery spell to be cast a second time. See rule [702.88](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-88), “Rebound.”
