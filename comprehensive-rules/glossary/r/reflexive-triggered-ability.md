# Reflexive Triggered Ability

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An ability that triggers based on actions taken earlier during a spell or ability’s resolution. See rule [603.12](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603-12).
