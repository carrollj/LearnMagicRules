Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that allows a creature to block an attacking creature with flying. See rule [702.17](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-17), “Reach.” See also [Flying](../f/flying.md).
