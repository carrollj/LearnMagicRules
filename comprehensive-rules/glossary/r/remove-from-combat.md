## Definition

Certain events can cause an attacking or blocking creature, or a planeswalker that’s being attacked, to be “removed from combat.” A permanent that’s removed from combat has no further involvement in that combat phase. See rule [506.4](../../5-turn-structure/506-combat-phase.md#rule-506-4).
