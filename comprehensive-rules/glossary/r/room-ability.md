Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A triggered ability that triggers whenever a player moves their venture marker into a room of a dungeon card. See rule [309](../../3-card-types/309-dungeons.md#rule-309), “Dungeons.”
