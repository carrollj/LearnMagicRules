Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets a player choose whether certain creatures enter the battlefield with haste or with a +1/+1 counter. See rule [702.136](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-136), “Riot.”
