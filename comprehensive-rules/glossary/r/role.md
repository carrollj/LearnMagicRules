## Definition

Roles are colorless enchantment tokens. Each one has the Aura and Role subtypes and the enchant creature ability. If a permanent has more than one Role attached to it controlled by the same player, each of those Roles except the one with the most recent timestamp is put into its owner’s graveyard. See rule [303.7](../../3-card-types/303-enchantments.md#rule-303-7) and rules [111.10j](../../1-game-concepts/111-tokens.md#rule-111-10j)–r.
