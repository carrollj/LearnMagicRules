Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A kind of ability. Activated abilities are written as “[Cost]: [Effect.] [Activation instructions (if any).]” See rule [113](../../1-game-concepts/113-abilities.md#rule-113), “Abilities,” and rule [602](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/602-activating-activated-abilities.md#rule-602), “Activating Activated Abilities.”
