## Definition

Everything that appears before the colon in an activated ability’s text. It must be paid to activate the ability. See rule [118](../../1-game-concepts/118-costs.md#rule-118), “Costs,” and rule [602](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/602-activating-activated-abilities.md#rule-602), “Activating Activated Abilities.”
