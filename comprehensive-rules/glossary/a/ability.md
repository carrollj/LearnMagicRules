## Definition

1. Text on an object that explains what that object does or can do.
2. An activated or triggered ability on the stack. This kind of ability is an object.
See rule [113](../../1-game-concepts/113-abilities.md#rule-113), “Abilities,” and section [6](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/README.md), “Spells, Abilities, and Effects.”
