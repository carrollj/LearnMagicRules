Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To put an activated ability onto the stack and pay its costs, so that it will eventually resolve and have its effect. See rule [602](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/602-activating-activated-abilities.md#rule-602), “Activating Activated Abilities.”
