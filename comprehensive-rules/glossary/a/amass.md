Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that gives you a Zombie Army creature token or grows an Army you already have. See rule [701.47](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-47), “Amass.”
