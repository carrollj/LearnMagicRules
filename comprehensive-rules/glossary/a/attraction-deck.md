# Attraction Deck

[Back to A entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

An optional deck of at least three (in limited play) or ten (in constructed play) Attraction cards that can be used to support play with some cards from the Unfinity expansion. See rule [717.2](../../7-additional-rules/717-attraction-cards.md#rule-717-2).
