Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

To turn a face-up ongoing scheme card face down and put it on the bottom of its owner’s scheme deck. See rule [701.33](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-33), “Abandon.”
