Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A word that precedes one of two abilities a permanent may enter the battlefield with. See rule [614.12b](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/614-replacement-effects.md#rule-614-12b).
