Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

The team who can attack during the combat phase of a multiplayer game using the shared team turns option. See rule [805](../../8-multiplayer-rules/805-shared-team-turns-option.md#rule-805), “Shared Team Turns Option.”
