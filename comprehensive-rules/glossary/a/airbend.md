# Airbend

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to A entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword action that exiles one or more permanents and/or spells. The owner of each card exiled with airbend may cast it from exile by paying {2} rather than paying its mana cost. See rule [701.65](../../7-additional-rules/701-keyword-actions.md#rule-701-65), “Airbend.”
