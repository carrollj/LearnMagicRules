## Definition

A Commander game that follows a modified version of the Archenemy rules. See rule [903](../../9-casual-variants/903-commander.md#rule-903), “Commander,” and rule [904](../../9-casual-variants/904-archenemy.md#rule-904), “Archenemy.”
