# Archenemy

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to A entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

1. A casual variant in which a team of players faces off against a single opponent strengthened with powerful scheme cards. See rule [904](../../9-casual-variants/904-archenemy.md#rule-904), “Archenemy.”
2. A player in an Archenemy game who is playing with a scheme deck.
