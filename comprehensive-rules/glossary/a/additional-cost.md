## Definition

A cost a spell may have that its controller may pay (or, in some cases, must pay) in addition to its mana cost to cast that spell. See rule [118](../../1-game-concepts/118-costs.md#rule-118), “Costs,” and rule [601](../../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/601-casting-spells.md#rule-601), “Casting Spells.”
