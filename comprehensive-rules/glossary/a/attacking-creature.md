# Attacking Creature

[Back to glossary index](../README.md)
[Back to A entries](README.md)

Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A creature that has either been declared as part of a legal attack during the combat phase (once all costs to attack, if any, have been paid), or a creature that has been put onto the battlefield attacking. It remains an attacking creature until it’s removed from combat or the combat phase ends, whichever comes first. See rule [508](../../5-turn-structure/508-declare-attackers-step.md#rule-508), “Declare Attackers Step.”
