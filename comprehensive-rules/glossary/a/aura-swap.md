Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules glossary (effective June 19, 2026)

## Definition

A keyword ability that lets you exchange an Aura on the battlefield with one in your hand. See rule [702.65](../../7-additional-rules/702-keyword-abilities.md#rule-702-65), “Aura Swap.”
