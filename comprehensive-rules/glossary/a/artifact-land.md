## Definition

A combination of artifact and land that’s subject to the rules for both. Artifact lands can only be played as lands, not cast as spells. See rule [301](../../3-card-types/301-artifacts.md#rule-301), “Artifacts,” and rule [305](../../3-card-types/305-lands.md#rule-305), “Lands.”
