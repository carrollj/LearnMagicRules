# 210. Defense

Chapter: 2. Parts of a Card
Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules (effective June 19, 2026)

## Rules

<a id="rule-210-1"></a>
210.1. Each battle card has a defense number printed in its lower right corner. This indicates its defense while it’s not on the battlefield, and it also indicates that the battle enters the battlefield with that many defense counters on it (see rule [310.4b](../3-card-types/310-battles.md#rule-310-4b)).
