# 200. General

Chapter: 2. Parts of a Card
Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules (effective June 19, 2026)

## Rules

<a id="rule-200-1"></a>
200.1. The parts of a card are name, mana cost, illustration, color indicator, type line, expansion symbol, text box, power and toughness, loyalty, defense, hand modifier, life modifier, illustration credit, legal text, and collector number. Some cards may have more than one of any or all of these parts.

<a id="rule-200-2"></a>
200.2. Some parts of a card are also characteristics of the object that has them. See rule [109.3](../1-game-concepts/109-objects.md#rule-109-3).

<a id="rule-200-3"></a>
200.3. Some objects that aren’t cards (tokens, copies of cards, and copies of spells) have some of the parts of a card, but only the ones that are also characteristics. See rule [111](../1-game-concepts/111-tokens.md#rule-111) and rule [707](../7-additional-rules/707-copying-objects.md#rule-707).
