# 212. Life Modifier

Chapter: 2. Parts of a Card
Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules (effective June 19, 2026)

## Rules

<a id="rule-212-1"></a>
212.1. Each vanguard card has a life modifier printed in its lower right corner. This is a number preceded by a plus sign, a number preceded by a minus sign, or a zero. This modifier is applied as the starting life total of the vanguard card’s owner is determined. See rule [103.4](../1-game-concepts/103-starting-the-game.md#rule-103-4).
