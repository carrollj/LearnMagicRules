# 503. Upkeep Step

Chapter: 5. Turn Structure
Source: Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules (effective June 19, 2026)

## Rules

<a id="rule-503-1"></a>
503.1. The upkeep step has no turn-based actions. Once it begins, the active player gets priority. (See rule [117](../1-game-concepts/117-timing-and-priority.md#rule-117), “Timing and Priority.”)

<a id="rule-503-1a"></a>
503.1a Any abilities that triggered during the untap step and any abilities that triggered at the beginning of the upkeep are put onto the stack before the active player gets priority; the order in which they triggered doesn’t matter. (See rule [603](../6-spells-abilities-and-effects/603-handling-triggered-abilities.md#rule-603), “Handling Triggered Abilities.”)

<a id="rule-503-2"></a>
503.2. If a spell states that it may be cast only “after [a player’s] upkeep step,” and the turn has multiple upkeep steps, that spell may be cast any time after the first upkeep step ends.
